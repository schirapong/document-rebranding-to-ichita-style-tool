## Company

- **Name**: ICHITA (wordmark is all caps: `ICHITA`)
- **Written form**: "Ichita" in body text, "ICHITA" in logo/wordmark context
- **Trademark**: ICHITA™
- **Tagline / Descriptor**: "Separation Technologies" (used with symbol mark)
- **Website**: ichita.co.th
- **Industry**: Industrial Separation & Purification Technologies
- **Brand Idea**: "Process Engineering Experts"
- **Brand Attributes**: Mechanical, Industrial, Efficiency, Process, Strength, Performance, Innovation, Expertise
- **Contact for brand questions**: wathaipan@srithepgroup.com

---

## Logo

### Wordmark
- Custom wordmark: `ICHITA™`
- Primary identifier, appears as sign-off on all communications
- Must never be altered, redrawn, or modified
- Clearspace: 50% of wordmark height on all sides

### Symbol
- Arrow-shaped "X" device derived from the angle of the 'A' in the wordmark
- Built around the concept of "Process" & "Innovation"
- Use only when Ichita context is already evident
- Clearspace: 50% of one arrow device height on all sides

### Logo Colourways
- Default: Blue Grey 03 (#263338) on light backgrounds
- On dark backgrounds: White
- Approved backgrounds: Blue Grey 01, Blue, Blue Grey 03, Blue Grey 02

## Typography

### Primary Font: Aeonik (also referenced as Aeonik Pro)
- **Weights**: Regular, Medium, Bold
- Used across ALL communications
- Modern, timeless aesthetic; structural and engineered feel
- Features: ligatures, fractions, case-sensitive punctuation, symbols, arrows

### Display Numerals: Betatron
- Used for display numbers only (e.g., section numbers, stats)
- Mechanical and industrial aesthetic
- Futuristic ideology, supports engineering/technology positioning

### Usage
- **Aeonik Regular**: Body copy, supporting text
- **Aeonik Medium**: Subtitles, emphasis
- **Aeonik Bold**: Headlines, key statements, accent text (often shown in Blue)

## Pattern

- Based on the concept of "process"
- Four variations: horizontal bars, vertical bars, graduated bars, dots/circles
- Can be used at various scales and in any brand colour
- Process-inspired graphic language: conveys movement, dynamism, depth
- Used as decorative elements on covers, backgrounds, posters

## Photography

### People
- Focus on customers, workforce, and their relationship
- Communicate: personal, relatable, reliable, assertive, confident
- Clean room / lab environments, industrial settings
- Blue uniforms/PPE prominent

### Operational
- Communicates scale of Ichita process
- Wide shots of factory/facility
- Close-up detail shots showing expertise and precision
- Equipment: stainless steel, pipes, industrial machinery

## Document Formatting Guidelines

When generating documents for Ichita, apply these rules:

1. **Company name**: Use "ICHITA" in headers/titles, "Ichita" in body text
2. **Primary colour accent**: Blue (#2978FF) for highlights, key words, links
3. **Heading colour**: Blue Grey 03 (#263338) or Blue Black (#171C21)
4. **Body text colour**: Blue Grey 03 (#263338)
5. **Background**: White or Blue Grey 01 (#CFD9DB)
6. **Font**: Aeonik (Regular for body, Medium for subtitles, Bold for headlines) — fallback: Avenir Next, then Calibri
7. **Section numbering**: Use Betatron-style display numerals where possible
8. **Footer**: Include "ichita.co.th" and Ichita wordmark or symbol
9. **Tone**: Professional, technical, innovative, confident
10. **Layout**: Clean, structured, generous whitespace, grid-based
