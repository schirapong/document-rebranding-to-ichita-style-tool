## About Us

ICHITA Co., Ltd. was established in 2002 as an Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (EPC) company specializing in liquid separation and biorefinery process technologies for sugar refineries. Over two decades of continuous innovation, ICHITA has grown into a trusted provider of advanced liquid separation and biorefinery process solutions across the food, beverage, and bioindustry sectors.

As the demands of the sugar refinery industry became increasingly sophisticated — with clients seeking greater product diversification — ICHITA was called upon to deliver total turnkey package solutions that ensure final product quality from end to end.

ICHITA is an affiliate company with **SRYA (Sriarayathep)**, which distributes advanced industrial technologies including ion exchange resin, membranes, and adsorbent solutions across Thailand, focusing on liquid separation, biorefinery processes, and environmental sustainability.

Today, ICHITA serves as a partner to manufacturers in sugar, sweetener, beverage, and food ingredient industries, delivering advanced liquid separation and biorefinery process technologies that drive efficiency, quality, and sustainability.

### OEM Process Solutions

Our OEM services cover a comprehensive range of process solutions:

- **Fruit Juice Clarification** — membrane-based clarification for beverage manufacturers

### Separation Technologies

We utilize industry-leading separation technologies including:

- Ion exchange resin systems
- Membrane microfiltration and ultrafiltration
- Nanofiltration and reverse osmosis (RO)
- Adsorbent media including activated carbon and Ecosorb technology
- Chromatography SMB (Simulated Moving Bed) modules

### Ion Exchange Services

For customers who purchase resin from ICHITA, we provide professional ion exchange removal and replacement services. Our trained staff manage every transaction with meticulous care to prevent contamination. In ultrapure water systems, our service is guaranteed for zero contamination.

## Innovation Highlights

### Membrane Technology for Sugar Derivatives

ICHITA has developed a revolutionary approach to purify sugar derivatives — including Isomalto-oligosaccharides (IMO) and Resistant Maltodextrin — using advanced membrane technology. By selecting membranes with precisely calibrated pore sizes, glucose passes through while retaining IMO and resistant maltodextrin content, achieving product purity exceeding 95%.

### Key Advantages

| Advantage | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Organic Certification | Without ion-exchange resin, products qualify for organic certification at premium pricing |
| Zero Solid Waste | Membrane technology eliminates cake and floc waste from traditional processes |
| Compact Footprint | Membrane systems complete primary decolorization independently, ideal for space-constrained facilities |
| Simple Operation | Fully automatic systems designed for ease of use without requiring specialized operators |
| Floc Prevention | Effective elimination of floc-forming particles that typical filters cannot remove |
